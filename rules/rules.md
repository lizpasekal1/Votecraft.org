# VoteCraft Coin (VC) — Rules & Math

## What is VC Coin?

VoteCraft Coin (VC) is a platform-only altruism currency. It is earned when a user pairs a game purchase with a nonprofit donation. VC is not a cryptocurrency and has no value outside the VoteCraft platform.

---

## The Purchase Bundle

Every transaction bundles a game + a donation into a single payment.

| Item | Price |
|---|---|
| Game (bundled price) | $2.00 |
| Donation (user's choice) | $10 / $20 / $30 / $50 |
| **Total** | **$12 / $22 / $32 / $52** |

The game is discounted from its regular price of $5.00 as an incentive to donate.

---

## Payment Distribution

| Recipient | Amount | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Nonprofit | 95% of donation | Goes directly to the chosen cause |
| VoteCraft | 5% of donation | Platform fee |
| Game | $2.00 flat | Fixed bundled game price |

**Example — $10 donation:**
- Nonprofit receives: $9.50
- VoteCraft receives: $0.50
- Game revenue: $2.00
- Total paid by user: $12.00

---

## Earning VC

Users earn VC based on their donation amount at a rate of **10 VC per $1 donated**.

| Donation | VC Earned |
|---|---|
| $10 | 100 VC |
| $20 | 200 VC |
| $30 | 300 VC |
| $50 | 500 VC |

---

## Redeeming VC

VC can be redeemed in two ways:

### Option A — Spend on VoteCraft Games (full value)
- **Rate:** 100 VC = $1.00 off
- **Cap:** Maximum 200 VC (= $2.00 off) per purchase
- **Minimum:** No minimum
- **Why capped:** Prevents users from getting games fully free; ensures repeat engagement

### Option B — Cash Out (half value)
- **Rate:** 100 VC = $0.50 cash
- **Minimum:** 500 VC required to cash out
- **Why half value:** Incentivizes platform loyalty; protects VoteCraft's margin
- **Why 500 VC minimum:** Requires at least a $50 donation before cash redemption is possible

---

## Profitability Model

VoteCraft keeps 5% of every donation. Cash redemptions pay out at 50% of face value, so VoteCraft breaks even on cash redemptions before accounting for:

- Users who never redeem (estimated 60–80% of VC goes unredeemed in typical loyalty programs)
- Users who redeem on games instead of cash (VoteCraft controls game pricing)
- Payment processing fees (~2.9% + $0.30 per transaction) must be covered by the 5% cut

**For VoteCraft to be profitable**, the blended redemption rate across all users must stay below 5% of total donations. The dual redemption structure (full value on platform, half value as cash) naturally steers users toward game redemption.

---

## Altruism Tags

In addition to VC, every purchase earns the user two **altruism tags** tied to the nonprofit's cause category. Tags:

- Are permanent and non-transferable
- Accumulate in the user's VC wallet
- Signal civic identity across the VoteCraft platform
- May unlock further discounts from partnered organizations aligned with the user's tag profile

Tags are awarded at the time of purchase and cannot be revoked.

---

## Entry Paths

Users can enter the purchase flow from either direction:

- **Support a cause** — pick a nonprofit first, then receive a game
- **Support a game** — pick a game first, then choose a cause to support

Both paths result in the same payment structure and VC/tag awards. The path only determines the order of steps in the UI.

---

## VC is Not Crypto

VC Coin is a loyalty points system, not a blockchain token or cryptocurrency. It has no market price, cannot be transferred between users, and is not subject to securities regulations. Its value is fixed and set by VoteCraft.

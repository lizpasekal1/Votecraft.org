4 "Risk Pool Simulator"

Interactive demo showing how insurance works - why larger pools are more stable and why "everyone in" makes costs predictable.

Small Company Pool
10 people 20% sick
Monthly Premium Per Person
$847
Try it: Click "Add Sick Person" to see how one person with high costs affects everyone's premium in a small pool. Then switch to a larger pool to see the difference.
Average Premium Costs
Kaiser Family Foundation Employer Health Benefits Survey 2024 - Annual family premium averages $24,479 ($847/month employer + employee share).
High-Cost Patient Impact
Peterson-KFF Health System Tracker - Top 5% of patients account for ~50% of healthcare spending. Average annual cost for chronic conditions: $36,000+.
Small vs. Large Pool Volatility
CMS Medical Loss Ratio Data - Small group markets show 2-3x premium volatility compared to large group markets.
Universal Pool Stability
CMS National Health Expenditure Data - Medicare per-capita spending grows more predictably (~4-5%/year) than private insurance (~6-8%/year).
Note: This simulator uses simplified models for educational purposes. Actual premium calculations involve many more factors including age rating, geography, and plan design.

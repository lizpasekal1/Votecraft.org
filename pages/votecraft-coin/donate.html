What is VoteCraft Coin?

VC is an altruism-based currency earned through civic action — donating, learning, volunteering. It has no cash value and can never be traded for real money. Instead, you spend it in the VoteCraft Emporium on civic guides, art packs, games, and more.

When you donate to VoteCraft, you earn VC as a thank-you. It's our way of recognizing your contribution to keeping democracy tools alive.

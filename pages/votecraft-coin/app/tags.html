How tags work

Each time you donate to a nonprofit through VoteCraft, you receive two altruism tags tied to that cause. The tags reflect the type of impact your donation supports and the nature of your contribution. Over time, your tag collection builds a unique civic profile.

Your tags are permanently attached to your account. They can never be revoked or removed. Wherever VoteCraft is used — games, donations, community events — your tags follow you and represent your civic identity.

As you accumulate tags, partnered organizations recognize you as an aligned supporter and offer you further discounts. For example, a nonprofit focused on voting rights may offer discounts to users with multiple "Voting Rights" tags.

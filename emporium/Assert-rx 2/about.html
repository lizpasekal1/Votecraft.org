ASSERT-Rx

About

Assert-rx is a self-practice tool built on 6 core assertiveness skills. Use it to prepare for real conversations — with honesty, not aggression.

The 6 Scenarios

  • Requests — Asking clearly for what you need
  • Boundaries — Protecting your well-being
  • Disagreements — Standing your ground respectfully
  • Needs — Expressing what matters to you
  • Clarification — Understanding before reacting
  • Compromise — Finding real middle ground

The 6 Skills

  1. What matters most here?
  2. What do you want to ask for?
  3. What are you willing to work with?
  4. What do you need to understand?
  5. How would you say no?
  6. What does this mean to you?

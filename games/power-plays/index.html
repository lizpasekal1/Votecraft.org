Quick Reference

Action Cards

  • Vote — State the color; all players vote with their highest number card in that color. The winner earns a lobby card.
  • Veto — Skip the next player's turn
  • Motion — Reverse play direction
  • +2 Inflation — Next player draws 2
  • 🎁 Gratuities — Give 1 card from your hand to any player

Countering

Play the same action type to cancel that action on you and earn lobby cards:

  • Veto — counter with any Veto (+1), or yellow Executive Veto (+2)
  • Motion — counter with any Motion (+1), or blue Legislative Motion (+2)
  • Inflation — counter with any Inflation (+1), or green Fed Reserve Inflation (+2)
  • Gratuities — counter with any Gratuities (+1), or red Judicial Gratuities (+2)

Lobby Cards

Uses your whole turn. Changes the active color + a bonus:

  • Bill — Draw 1 yourself OR force an opponent to draw 1
  • Court Case — Choose a player to skip their turn + ban all Vote cards for one round

Winning

Power winner: the first to play all their cards.
Plays winner: the player with the most lobby cards when the power winner is reached. This can be the same person.
